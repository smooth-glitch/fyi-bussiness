Common Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We have shipped bats to the UK, Australia, USA, and UAE. Shipping costs vary by location, but we ensure secure, damage-proof packaging.

Can I customize the handle shape?

Absolutely. Whether you prefer Oval, Round, or Semi-Oval, we customize the handle to fit your grip perfectly.

What is the warranty?

We offer a warranty on the handle for 6 months. While willow is a natural product and minor cracks are normal, we stand by the quality of our pressing and construction.

How long does delivery take?

For custom orders, the entire process from consultation to delivery typically takes 2-4 weeks. Ready-to-ship stock can be dispatched within 48 hours.

Do you offer knocking-in services?

Yes, every FYI bat comes with optional professional knocking-in and oiling so it's ready for the nets straight out of the box.

What if I don't like the bat?

We share detailed photos and videos of your specific bat for approval before dispatch. If there is a genuine manufacturing defect upon arrival, we will replace it.
